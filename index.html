ABOUT ME

Hi, I’m Mickel Simenti, a UI/UX designer with a passion for
creating clean, simple, and impactful designs. My inspiration
comes from the dynamic energy of hip-hop culture, which
helps me find a balance between creativity and structure.
With one year of experience as a freelancer, I specialize in
crafting intuitive and user-friendly interfaces

DESIGN PHILOSOPHY

I believe that simplicity is the ultimate form of
sophistication. My design approach is inspired by hip-hop
culture, where rhythm and balance create something
powerful yet effortless. I focus on clean, minimalistic
designs that prioritize usability and visual appeal,
ensuring a smooth and engaging user experience.
